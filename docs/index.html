Why Kairos?

While tools like Cursor and Copilot are excellent assistants, Kairos operates at a higher level—as a supervisor.

  • • Proactive vs. Reactive: Kairos doesn't just respond to prompts; it proactively manages the project's context.
  • • Long-Term Memory: It builds a comprehensive Knowledge Graph of your project.
  • • Autonomous Learning: Kairos is a self-improving system that gets better over time.
